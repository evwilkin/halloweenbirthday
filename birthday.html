Emily & Colin

A Halloween Tale

Born on Halloween, they were a couple dolls who grew up inseperable.

Unfortunately, it was quickly discovered that rather than milk and baby food, these twins of terror were zombie babies with a taste for blood!

They were soon committed to baby prison, for both their own protection and that of those around them.

Growing up in captivity, they learned to hone their skills to protect themselves.

Eventually they hit their teen years, starting a Raffi cover band.

Today they'd like you to think that they've grown into two responsible adults...

...but we know better!
